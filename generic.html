  • Stock Price Prediction

Stock price prediction involves forecasting the future value of a company’s stock based on historical data, market trends, and various financial indicators. Analysts use methods like technical analysis, which studies price patterns, and fundamental analysis, which evaluates company performance, earnings, and economic conditions. I employed the LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) algorithm using TensorFlow and Keras libraries. The dataset was split into 65% training data and the remaining for testing. To enhance model performance, the MinMax Scaler was used to normalize the data, ensuring values fell within a consistent range. LSTM was chosen for its ability to capture long-term dependencies in time-series data. This approach aims to predict future stock prices by learning patterns from historical price trends and market behavior.

Skills: Python, Machine Learning, Analytical Thinking, Data Visualization, Problem Solving
Libraries Used: Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib, Tensorflow, Keras, sklearn

  • Flight Price Prediction - EDA and FE

In flight price prediction, Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) involves understanding the dataset by visualizing and summarizing key features such as departure times, airline carriers, flight duration, and ticket prices. EDA helps identify patterns, outliers, and correlations that affect prices. Feature Engineering (FE) is the process of creating new variables to improve model accuracy, such as extracting features like time of booking, day of the week, or seasonality. Both EDA and FE are essential for preparing data and extracting meaningful insights for accurate flight price predictions.

Skills: Python, Machine Learning, Analytical Thinking, Data Visualization, EDA and FE Techniques
Libraries Used: Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib, sklearn
